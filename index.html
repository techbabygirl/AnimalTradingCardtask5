Name: CAT

Scientific Name : Felis catus


Description : The domestic cat, scientifically known as Felis catus, is a small carnivorous mammal cherished for its playful nature and companionship. With retractable claws, keen senses, and an independent spirit, cats are agile hunters. Known for grooming habits and distinctive vocalizations, these feline companions have formed strong bonds with humans throughout history, making them one of the most popular and beloved pets globally.
